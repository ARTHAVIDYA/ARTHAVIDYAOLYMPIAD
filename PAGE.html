Olympiad Programs

National Science Olympiad (NSO)

The National Science Olympiad is one of the most popular science competitions in India.

  • Eligibility: Class 1-12
  • Started In: 1999
  • Recognized By: SOF, affiliated with CBSE & ICSE

International Mathematics Olympiad (IMO)

The IMO is one of the oldest and most prestigious mathematics competitions in the world.

  • Eligibility: Class 1-12
  • Started In: 1959
  • Recognized By: Ministry of Education, Govt. of India

Indian Computing Olympiad (ICO)

ICO is an Olympiad in computer science and programming, serving as a gateway to the International Olympiad in Informatics (IOI).

  • Eligibility: Class 8-12
  • Started In: 2004
  • Recognized By: IARCS, Ministry of Electronics and IT

National Cyber Olympiad (NCO)

NCO aims to enhance students' skills in information technology and internet literacy.

  • Eligibility: Class 1-12
  • Started In: 2000
  • Recognized By: SOF

International General Knowledge Olympiad (IGKO)

The IGKO tests students' knowledge of general topics across history, geography, sports, etc.

  • Eligibility: Class 1-12
  • Started In: 2010
  • Recognized By: SOF

Unified Council Olympiads (NSTSE & UCO)

The National Level Science Talent Search Exam (NSTSE) and the Unified Cyber Olympiad (UCO) are designed to assess students' abilities in science and IT.

  • Eligibility: Class 1-12 (NSTSE), Class 2-12 (UCO)
  • Started In: 2001 (NSTSE), 2003 (UCO)
  • Recognized By: Unified Council

SilverZone Olympiads

SilverZone conducts multiple Olympiads in subjects like Science, Mathematics, English, and more.

  • Eligibility: Class 1-12
  • Started In: 2009
  • Recognized By: SilverZone Foundation

Indian Talent Olympiad (ITO)

ITO offers a variety of Olympiads across multiple subjects including Science, Mathematics, English, and Social Studies.

  • Eligibility: Class 1-12
  • Started In: 2014
  • Recognized By: Indian Talent Foundation

Kishore Vaigyanik Protsahan Yojana (KVPY)

KVPY is a national-level scholarship program that tests students' scientific aptitude.

  • Eligibility: Class 11 & 12 (Science stream)
  • Started In: 1999
  • Recognized By: Department of Science & Technology, Govt. of India

Green Olympiad by TERI

Green Olympiad aims to create awareness about environmental issues and sustainability among students.

  • Eligibility: Class 4-12
  • Started In: 1999
  • Recognized By: TERI, Government of India
